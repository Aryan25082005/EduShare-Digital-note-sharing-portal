About EduShare

A student-built platform that makes quality study materials freely accessible to every engineering student.

🎯

Our Mission

To eliminate information inequality among engineering students by creating a centralised, free hub of peer-reviewed notes for every branch and semester.

💡

Why We Built This

Finding reliable notes before exams was always a struggle. EduShare was born out of that frustration — a platform where seniors help juniors and everyone benefits together.

🔒

Quality & Trust

Every upload is tagged by branch, semester, and subject. Our admin team reviews flagged content, and the community rating system surfaces the best notes automatically.
