How to Upload

  1. Upload your PDF to Google Drive or Dropbox
  2. Set sharing to "Anyone with the link can view"
  3. Copy the shareable link
  4. Paste the link below and submit!

Tip: For Google Drive, use "Get link" → "Anyone with the link" → Copy

Make sure the link is publicly accessible (Anyone with link can view)
